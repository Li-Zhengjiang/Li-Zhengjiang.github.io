This talk presents our study of how the vortex state of high-energy electrons can be detected through elastic electron scattering.

The main idea is to identify observable signatures that can distinguish vortex states from ordinary plane-wave states, which is important for future experimental work on high-energy particles carrying orbital angular momentum.
