I am a Physics PhD student at Nanyang Technological University. My research interests include cold atoms, quantum information, quantum simulation, and related topics in high-energy physics.

My current work focuses on problems at the interface of quantum many-body physics, AMO physics, and quantum technologies. I am also interested in how theoretical ideas can connect to concrete experimental platforms.

This website collects my publications, talks, teaching experience, and notes. I will continue to update it with research outputs, presentation materials, and study notes over time.

If you would like to get in touch, please feel free to contact me by email.
